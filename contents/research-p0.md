- Sediment toxicokinetic-toxicodynamic (TKTD) model describes how **<u>metals</u>** in sediments are **<u>taken up, accumulated, and cause toxic effects</u>** in organisms **<u>over time</u>**.
- **<u>A quantitative description</u>** of these dynamic processes includes parameters such as $k_{\mathrm{u}}$ and $k_{\mathrm{f}}$ representing uptake rates, $k_{\mathrm{e}}$ representing elimination rate, and $S(t)$ representing survival rate.
